About Us

Welcome to the best and most friendly dog kennel in East Stroudsburg, PA! Our kennel is run by a passionate dog owner who has a deep love for pets. We understand that your furry friends are more than just animals; they are beloved members of your family. That's why we provide a safe and loving environment for your pets, ensuring they receive the care and attention they deserve. Whether your dog is staying with us for a day or a week, we treat them like one of our own, giving them plenty of playtime, exercise, and affection. Your pet's happiness and well-being are our top priorities, and we strive to make their stay with us as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.
